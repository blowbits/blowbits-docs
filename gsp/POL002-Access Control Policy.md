# 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the access control framework for the Blowbits Solutions LLP GST Suvidha Provider (GSP) platform.

The objective of this policy is to ensure that access to applications, systems, infrastructure and information assets is granted, managed and revoked through controlled processes based on operational responsibilities and business requirements.

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to all users, administrators, authorised applications, infrastructure components and supporting systems associated with the operation and maintenance of the Blowbits Solutions LLP GSP platform.

Supporting procedures, technical standards and implementation evidence are maintained separately.

# 3. Access Control Framework

Access to GSP resources is governed through controlled identification, authentication and authorisation processes.

The framework is established to:

- Ensure access is granted only to authorised users and applications.
- Restrict access according to assigned responsibilities.
- Protect systems and information from unauthorised access.
- Support periodic review of access permissions.
- Maintain accountability for access to information assets.

# 4. Access Control Principles

The GSP platform operates according to the following principles:

- Every user and application is assigned an appropriate identity before access is granted.
- Access permissions are limited to operational requirements.
- Administrative privileges are restricted to authorised personnel.
- Authentication is completed before access to protected resources is permitted.
- Access rights are reviewed periodically and updated when required.
- Access activities are supported by operational monitoring and logging.

# 5. Access Lifecycle Management

Access rights are managed throughout their operational lifecycle.

The lifecycle includes:

- Provisioning of access.
- Modification of access permissions.
- Periodic review of assigned privileges.
- Temporary suspension where required.
- Permanent revocation when access is no longer authorised.

# 6. Compliance

Compliance with this policy is supported through documented procedures, operational practices, access reviews and implementation evidence.

Detailed operational processes and technical controls are maintained separately to support the effective implementation of this policy.

## Standard Statement

This policy establishes the access control principles governing the Blowbits Solutions LLP GST Suvidha Provider (GSP) platform. Supporting procedures, operational records, configuration evidence and implementation artefacts are maintained separately in accordance with organisational procedures.
