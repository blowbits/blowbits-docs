# 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the incident response framework for the Blowbits Solutions LLP GST Suvidha Provider (GSP) platform.

The objective of this policy is to ensure that information security incidents affecting the GSP platform are identified, reported, assessed, managed and resolved through structured operational processes to minimise operational impact and support the continued delivery of GSP services.

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to information security incidents affecting applications, infrastructure, networks, operational processes and supporting services associated with the Blowbits Solutions LLP GST Suvidha Provider (GSP) platform.

Supporting procedures, technical standards and implementation evidence are maintained separately.

# 3. Incident Response Framework

Information security incidents are managed through structured operational processes.

The framework is established to:

- Support timely identification and reporting of security incidents.
- Assess the operational impact of reported incidents.
- Coordinate appropriate containment and recovery activities.
- Restore affected services through controlled operational procedures.
- Maintain records of incident handling activities.
- Promote continual improvement through post-incident review.

# 4. Incident Response Principles

The GSP platform operates according to the following principles:

- Information security incidents are reported through established operational channels.
- Incidents are evaluated according to their operational impact.
- Appropriate containment measures are implemented to minimise service disruption.
- Recovery activities are coordinated through authorised operational procedures.
- Incident handling activities are documented and retained.
- Lessons identified during incident response support continual improvement.

# 5. Incident Management Process

Incident response activities are managed through structured operational procedures.

The operational framework includes:

- Incident identification.
- Incident reporting.
- Assessment and classification.
- Containment and recovery.
- Service restoration.
- Post-incident review and closure.

# 6. Compliance

Compliance with this policy is supported through documented procedures, operational practices, incident records and implementation evidence.

Detailed operational processes and technical controls are maintained separately to support the effective implementation of this policy.

## Standard Statement

This policy establishes the incident response principles governing the Blowbits Solutions LLP GST Suvidha Provider (GSP) platform. Supporting procedures, operational records, configuration evidence and implementation artefacts are maintained separately in accordance with organisational procedures.
