# 1. Purpose

This document maintains current internal role assignments, contact details and external communication points used for operational support, GSTN coordination, security incident reporting and regulatory communication associated with the Blowbits Solutions LLP GST Suvidha Provider (GSP) platform.

Policies and procedures define responsibilities by role. This directory identifies the current people and communication channels for those roles.

# 2. Scope

This directory applies to:

- Internal GSP operational, security, compliance and management contacts.
- External GSTN communication channels.
- External statutory, infrastructure and operational support contacts.
- Emergency and security incident communication.

# 6. Communication Channels

Official external communication may be carried out through one or more authorised channels:

- Official email.
- GSTN helpdesk or support portal.
- Telephone.
- Scheduled meetings.
- CERT-In Reporting Channel, where applicable.
- Cloud service provider support portal or support channel.
- Other communication channels designated by the relevant external organisation.

# 7. Availability and Updates

Designated contacts shall ensure that relevant communication channels remain available for operational support, security incident handling and regulatory matters.

Changes to role assignments, phone numbers, email addresses or external contact details shall be updated promptly to maintain the accuracy of this directory.

# 8. Review

This directory shall be reviewed at least annually and whenever there is a change in personnel, role assignment, external contact channel or communication responsibility.
