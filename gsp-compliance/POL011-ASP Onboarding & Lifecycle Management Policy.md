# 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the framework for onboarding, lifecycle management and governance of Application Service Providers (ASPs) authorised to access the Blowbits Solutions LLP GST Suvidha Provider (GSP) platform.

The objective of this policy is to ensure that ASP access is provisioned, managed and, where necessary, suspended or revoked through controlled processes that protect the security, integrity and reliability of GSP services while supporting applicable GSTN operational and security requirements.

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to all ASPs authorised to access the Blowbits Solutions LLP GST Suvidha Provider (GSP) platform.

Supporting procedures, technical standards and implementation evidence are maintained separately.

# 3. ASP Governance Framework

Access to GSP services is governed through structured operational processes.

The framework is established to:

- Verify the identity of ASPs before access is provisioned.
- Ensure ASPs satisfy applicable operational and security requirements.
- Maintain authorised ASP records.
- Govern ASP access throughout its operational lifecycle.
- Protect GSP services from unauthorised or non-compliant access.

# 4. ASP Governance Principles

The GSP platform operates according to the following principles:

- ASP access shall be provisioned only after successful onboarding and validation.
- Each ASP shall be assigned an independent identity and access profile.
- ASP access shall be limited to authorised operational requirements.
- ASPs shall maintain compliance with applicable GSTN operational and security requirements throughout their engagement.
- Security compliance evidence, including applicable ISO/IEC 27001 certification and CERT-In empanelled Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) reports, shall be verified in accordance with applicable GSTN requirements.
- ASP access may be modified, suspended or revoked through authorised administrative processes where operational or security requirements are not met.

# 5. ASP Lifecycle Management

ASP lifecycle activities are governed through structured operational processes.

The governance framework includes:

- ASP onboarding.
- Identity verification.
- Security and compliance verification.
- Access provisioning.
- Periodic review.
- Modification of authorised access.
- Suspension or revocation of ASP access.

# 6. Compliance

Compliance with this policy is supported through documented procedures, operational practices, lifecycle records and implementation evidence.

Detailed operational processes and technical controls are maintained separately to support the effective implementation of this policy and applicable GSTN requirements.
